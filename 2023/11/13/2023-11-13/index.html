目的

自我感觉逻辑混乱，思考问题没方向。虽然每个领域有每个领域的知识，并且思考某个领域的问题总是需要这个领域知识来指引。但是我曾经听过或者遇到过有希望作为所有领域公共知识的知识，我浅浅了解过的：老四论(System theory, …), 新四论, ZFC, 数理逻辑, 类型理论, 范畴论. 前两个是广义上的万物理论, 后四者是数学领域的”万物理论”. “虽然到了这个年纪还追求这种”屠龙之术”未免显得太幼稚”,但是哪怕一点点的可能也会让我心生向往. 毕竟, 论期望(E)来说, 这件事也不比其他的所谓很重要的事情更加不重要. 所以, 我打算搞明白 数学四论的关系. 给我自己一个答案.

  • 正在搞清楚为什么lean的语法强大到能表达数学. Now at propositional logic in lean.

https://lean-lang.org/logic_and_proof/

“Symbolic logic is not meant to replace ordinary mathematical language, and you should not use symbols like ∧∧ and ∨∨ in ordinary mathematical proofs any more than you would use them in place of the words “and” and “or” in letters home to your parents.” - from logic and proof, lean-lang.org

Three main languages:

  • symbolic logic: precise and complicated
  • ordinary mathematical language(natural language): high level
  • computational implementations in interactive proof assistants(Lean4, for example): a compromise of the two extremes above

“This book also aims to show you how mathematics is built up from fundamental concepts. Logic provides the rules of the game, and then we work our way up from properties of sets, relations, functions, and the natural numbers to elementary number theory, combinatorics, and properties of the real numbers. The last chapter rounds out the story with a discussion of axiomatic foundations.” - from logic and proof, lean-lang.org. Wonderful !
